Hello There

We Are Lumintu Logic

Lumintu Logic is an IT company that originates from Yogyakarta, Indonesia. The word Lumintu itself means continuous service in Javanese. Founded in 2009, Lumintu Logic started with the deed of establishment as CV (Commanditaire Venootschap). In 2022, Lumintu Logic upgraded its deed to PT (Perseroan Terbatas) mainly due to its expanding business area.

Our commitment to continuous service drives us to stay ahead in the ever-evolving IT industry. We offer unlimited IT consultation services, addressing all your needs, from desktop applications to web environments and emerging frameworks. Whatever your IT challenges or ideas, we are here to support you. To ensure the reliability and availability of our services, we are also maintaining the availability of services by investing in new infrastructure, which we prioritize as the home for our SaaS (Software as a Service) offerings..
